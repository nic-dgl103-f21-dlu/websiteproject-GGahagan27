About Moon's

At Moon's Records, we are passionate about music. We believe that vinyl records deliver a superior listening experience, which is why we carry vinyl exclusively. We carry a wide selection of records in all genres, Whatever your musical tastes, there is something for everyone at the store. For more information on what vinyl we carry, please visit our New and Used Vinyl page. Moon's Records is also devoted to locally crafted artisan products. We carry a wide selection of locally made clothing, jewelry, and art. We also carry our own Moon's Merch and have a variety of hats, sweatshirts, hoodies, and tees for folks of all ages.
