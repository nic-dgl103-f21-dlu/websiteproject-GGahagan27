Welcome to Cumberland's New Skateshop!

Check Us Out

We are pleased to serve the skateboarding community in Cumberland and the greater Comox Vally! Come check out our new skate shop in the same building as Moon’s Records. We carry a variety of decks by BC brands as well as We’ve got decks, trucks, wheels, grip tape, and gear. Whatever you need, we’ve got you covered. We also sponsor youth skate camps called Drop into Life (link). Whether you or your child wants to shred street, ramp, or bowl, our camps provide a safe space for youth to learn the fundamentals of skateboarding in a positive environment. The camps are taught by seasoned and certified skateboard instructors.

Sign Up for Drop in To Life

Drop in to life Youth skatecamps run from June-September at the Cumberland Skatepark. The camps are for kids 7-18 year's old and are taught by cerified skateboard instructors. Helmets are mandatory. Check the schedule for dates and times, and use the sign up button below!
