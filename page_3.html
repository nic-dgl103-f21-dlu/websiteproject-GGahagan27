The High Quadra Ramblers

Taking their name from a street in Victoria, BC, the High Quadra Ramblers bring their inspired take on high-energy music that draws influences from their Cape Breton Celtic roots to the West Coast. The Ramblers combine celt, bluegrass, ragtime, metis, and classical and put their own spin on traditional music.
